What's the new American dream? before, it was write the great American novel, or meet a rich girl to marry. Or be a scientist! Now, I feel there is no American dream. America is a bunch of corporations that I hold stock in. Line must go up! I want to know that my future is secure, my belly won't go empty. I want to know that your belly isn't empty, or that your feet aren't frozen.

America is the land of the free and the home of the brave. Be brave! Be brave enough to try! be brave enough to fail. Wait, don't fail, we hate losers and don't take care of our own. We laugh at someone else's pain because fuck them. But we all pay taxes. I do. Taxes are what makes this shit work. I don't agree with all of the shit my taxes get spent on, but you know what?  That's why I pay taxes! so I *get* to not care about that bullshit. 
The real American dream was to get a job (anything where someone gives you money is a job), a a house, a partner, if you wanted, kids, if you wanted. And that you'd be safe. The first thing in life, liberty and the pursuit of happiness is *life* god damnit. But what is life in the face of goon squads kidnapping people on our streets. What is liberty, if I can't shittalk online? Trump's goon's gonna poke around my place of work and intimidated people? I'm sure my local politicians are corrupt, because the FBI comes around every 10 years and finds somebody but the lesson shouldn't be that politicians are corrupt, the lesson should be that I'm lucky enough that they only have to come around every ten years to find one! 

Where my politician that's going to run on the one power tool battery pack standard? Where's my politician that's going to run on no daylight savings time? Instead, we get bland corporate not saying anything, or Elon.

I don't have a point, thank you for reading.
